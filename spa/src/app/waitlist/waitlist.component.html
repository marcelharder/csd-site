Surgical Waitinglist Application

Motivation:
This application was developed to streamline a referral for cardiac surgery. All the information
about the patient is presented to the surgeon. This information includes the angiogram, echo's and
other additional investigations that are relevant to the referral.
With all the information about a potential surgical case in one place, the surgeon can quickly estimate
the surgical risk and envisioned benefit of the surgical procedure.
The main benefit of this application is the significant reduction of administrative tasks that
accompany a referral for cardiac surgery.
